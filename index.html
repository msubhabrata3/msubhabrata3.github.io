About Me

Hello! I'm a software engineer with a passion for photography and videography.
When I'm not immersed in the world of coding, you can find me capturing moments through the lens.
I am also a part-time chef at my kitchen. My love for visual storytelling extends to my interest in cooking, where I experiment with flavors and create culinary delights.
But if I had chosen a different career path and not become a software developer, I can envision myself embracing the world of stand-up comedy.
Comedy provides a unique avenue for self-expression, and I believe the joy of making people laugh is a fulfilling alternative to the technical challenges of software development.
The ability to craft jokes, observe the lighter side of life, and share those moments with others is a compelling alternative profession that I would have explored with enthusiasm.
Balancing the technical intricacies of software development with the creativity of visual arts and the joy of making people laugh, I lead a diverse and fulfilling life.
